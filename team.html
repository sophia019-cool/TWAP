The People Behind the Project

Who We Are

We're student-athletes who believe that mental health is just as important as physical performance. This is who we are!

Co-Founder & Karate Athlete

Sophia

Hi, I'm Sophia, and I'm doing this because I care deeply about athlete mental health and the pressure athletes often face that isn't always visible. I want to help bring more awareness to what goes on beyond performance, and contribute to a space where athletes feel supported, understood, and able to speak openly about their experiences.

Co-Founder & Water Polo Athlete

Caity

Hi, I'm Caity. I'm a student-athlete and water polo player who's passionate about supporting other athletes both in and out of the pool. Being part of a team has shown me how important it is to balance mental health, physical wellness, and academics — and I want to help others find that balance too. Through this, I hope to advocate for better resources, stronger connections, and a healthier mindset for all student-athletes.

Co-Founder & Basketball / Tennis Athlete

Addison

Hi! My name is Addison Davis and I play basketball and tennis at Campolindo. I also participate in the spring musical at Campo. I am doing this because I think it is super important to bring the mental health of athletes to the forefront of teams and coaches.

Co-Founder & Swimmer

Oliver

My name is Oliver Winter. I've been swimming for 10 years, and I'm doing this because one of my teammates passed away and I want people to be aware that they can talk to others if they need help.

Co-Founder & Cross Country / Track Athlete

Vasy

My name is Vasy Pridatko and I run cross country and track at Campolindo. I am working with TWAP as I understand the never-ending challenges of being a student-athlete.
